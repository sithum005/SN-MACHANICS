Our Repair Services

Detailed information about the specialized repair services we offer for various industrial and domestic sewing machines.

Singer Single Needle

Complete service and repair for Singer Single Needle lockstitch machines. We handle timing adjustments, motor repairs, and tension calibration.

Common repairs include:

  • Hook timing adjustment
  • Feed dog replacement
  • Motor speed control fix
  • Oil pump service

Request Repair

Zoje Industrial Machine

Expert diagnostics and repair for Zoje heavy-duty industrial machines. From direct drive systems to mechanical linkages, we ensure your machine runs smoothly at high speeds.

Our expertise:

  • Electronic control box repair
  • Needle bar height adjustment
  • Automatic thread trimmer calibration

Request Repair

Baby Overlock

Precision repair for domestic and semi-industrial Baby Overlock machines. We fix threading issues, blade sharpening, and loop timing.

Services offered:

  • Lower/Upper looper timing
  • Knife sharpening/replacement
  • Tension balancing (4-thread / 3-thread)

Request Repair

Zoje 5 Overlock

Specialized repair for 5-thread safety stitch industrial overlockers. We handle complex differential feed adjustments and oil circulation issues.

Key Fixes:

  • Chain stitch looper timing
  • Differential feed calibration
  • Gasket replacement and leak fixing
